Problem Scenario

The Challenge

  • Customers have no centralized way to report issues
  • Support teams lose track of ticket status and priority
  • No visibility into recurring issues or sentiment trends
  • Manual triage wastes time and delays resolution

Our Solution

  • Web portal for customers to submit support tickets
  • Dashboard with real-time stats by status & priority
  • Inline status & priority management for agents
  • AI-powered ticket summarization & sentiment analysis
"A simple customer support portal where users can submit product issues, track resolution status, and where AI can analyze patterns to improve product quality."

System Architecture

⚛️

Frontend

React + Vite + Tailwind CSS

Data Model & APIs

Single entity: Ticket — represents a customer support request
FieldTypeDescription
idINTEGER PKAuto-incrementing unique identifier
titleTEXT NOT NULLBrief summary of the issue
descriptionTEXTDetailed explanation of the problem
priorityENUMlow | medium | high | critical
statusENUMopen | in_progress | resolved | closed
customer_nameTEXTName of the person reporting
customer_emailTEXTContact email for follow-up
created_atDATETIMEWhen the ticket was submitted

REST API Endpoints

GET /api/tickets — List all (with filters)
GET /api/tickets/:id — Single ticket
POST /api/tickets — Create new ticket
PUT /api/tickets/:id — Update ticket
GET /api/tickets/stats — Dashboard stats
POST /api/tickets/:id/summarize — AI analysis

How AI & CRM Systems Could Use This Data

Connecting support data to intelligent product workflows
IMPLEMENTED

AI Ticket Summarization

GPT analyzes each ticket to produce a summary, suggest resolution steps, and detect customer sentiment (frustrated/angry/neutral).

CONCEPT

Recurring Bug Detection

NLP clustering across tickets to identify patterns — e.g., "5 tickets this week about login failures" → auto-create a product issue.

CONCEPT

Smart Ticket Routing

AI classifies tickets by category (billing, technical, feature request) and routes to the appropriate team automatically.

CONCEPT

Customer Health Scoring

Aggregate sentiment and ticket frequency per customer to predict churn risk and trigger proactive outreach.

How DevRev Could Integrate

DevRev's convergence of support and product development is a natural fit. Support tickets from this portal could feed into DevRev's work items — linking customer issues directly to engineering sprints, auto-prioritizing based on customer impact, and closing the loop when fixes ship.
